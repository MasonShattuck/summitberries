Who We Are

About Summit Berries LLC

Our Vision

At Summit Berries LLC, our name is more than a brand — it’s a vision inspired by Elizabeth Barrett Browning: “Every bush is aflame with the fire of God, but only those who see take off their shoes. The rest just pick the berries.”

“Berries” symbolize the small details of everyday life, while “Summit” represents rising above the ordinary. Together, Summit Berries means bringing mindfulness, excellence, and care into property management.

Background

Our Story

Summit Berries was founded with a mission to elevate property management in Park City. We believe every detail matters — from snow removal to pool maintenance — and that every client deserves personalized care.

What We Believe

We go beyond routine maintenance — Summit Berries provides full-service care for Park City homeowners. Whether it’s tackling heavy winter storms or preparing your pool for summer, our team ensures your property thrives in every season.

As a locally owned business, we believe in giving back to the communities we serve. We support local initiatives, environmental efforts, and projects that keep Utah a great place to live.
